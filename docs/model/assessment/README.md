- Capability (Pillars)
- Safety & Readiness (Stage Gates)
- Operating Mode (Levels)

## How the Assessment Works

Overall maturity is determined by the interaction of:

1. **Capability Pillars** — what the organization can reliably do  
2. **Maturity Levels** — how work is actually performed  
3. **Stage Gates** — whether operating at that level is safe

## How to Use This Framework

1. Assess capability across all pillars  
2. Determine the dominant operating level  
3. Evaluate stage gate readiness  
4. Identify mismatches and risks  
5. Use results to guide transformation planning  

This framework is intended for facilitated organizational assessment, not automated scoring.

---

## Important Notes

- Organizations may operate at multiple levels simultaneously  
- Higher autonomy without supporting capability increases risk  
- Pilot teams do not determine enterprise maturity  
- Claims of advanced maturity should be domain-specific
