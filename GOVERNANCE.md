# Governance

## Maintainer Model

This project uses a lightweight maintainer model.

- Lead Maintainer: Project creator
- Core Maintainers: Trusted contributors with merge rights
- Community Contributors: Anyone submitting issues or pull requests

## Decision Making

- Minor changes: Maintainer approval
- Major changes: Maintainer plus community discussion
- Breaking changes: Version bump required

## Becoming a Maintainer

Contributors may be invited based on:

- Quality of contributions
- Expertise
- Sustained engagement
- Alignment with project goals

## Conflict Resolution

Maintainers aim for consensus. If consensus cannot be reached, the Lead Maintainer makes the final decision.
